Why do we do thisÔºü

That day, a high school student who often felt anxious and irritable due to the low efficiency of her studies in big cities walked into a dilapidated school in the deep mountains of Guizhou. She originally wanted to escape the suffocating feeling brought by the "rat race" and go into the deep mountains to experience perhaps a more simple lifestyle...

The density of mosquitoes in the tiny toilet is beyond description. The so-called blackboard is merely a thin layer of putty pasted on top. The cramped teaching building houses all the children from kindergarten to the third year of junior high school. Their hands, which should be tender and smooth, are covered with blisters accumulated from long-term farm work.

When the astonishing scenes of life before one's eyes keep emerging one after another, a pair of eyes that hold back tears out of genuine heartache meet a pair of clean and strong eyes that still have beautiful expectations for the outside world despite having experienced the vicissitudes of life early on.
The meaning of this website is thus born~

We hope

Through this website, we aim to help teenagers like her who are under increasing study pressure every day due to failing to complete their plans. Moreover, through the efforts of these teenagers, we strive to assist the children in the mountains!
